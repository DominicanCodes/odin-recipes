Tostones

Description

This easy vegetarian recipe will have you coming back time and time again. This is twice fried plantains, seasoned with a bit of salt to add extra flavor. A classic Dominican dish.

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel plantains and cut into 1 inch thick slices
  2. Fry them in cooking oil for about 3-4 minutes each side
  3. Transfer to plate with paper towel
  4. Use something to flatten them into about half or their original size
  5. Return pressed plantains to the hot oil
  6. Fry until crisp around the edges, about 2 minutes on each side
  7. Remove from oil and place on a plate lined with paper towels
  8. Sprinkle with salt and serve hot

Buen Provecho! (Enjoy your meal!)
